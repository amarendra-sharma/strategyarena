Arena
Signaling
You're a job-market worker who privately knows your own productivity type, and you must choose an education signal to send to an employer who can't see your type directly. Education costs you something, and the employer sets your wage based only on what they can observe. Think about what your signal communicates — and what it costs you to send it — given who you actually are.
You get two attempts. After finishing your first attempt, check your grade in the app. If you're happy with it, you're done — only use your second attempt if you want to try to improve.
